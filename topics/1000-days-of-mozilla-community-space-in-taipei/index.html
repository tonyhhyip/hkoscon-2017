Talk

1000 days of Mozilla Community Space in Taipei

Irvin Chen

Volunteers of MozTW had already running Mozilla Community Space Taipei "摩茲工寮" for 2.5 years. What have we done, how do we manage it, and what we have learned. If you're interesting in running your own hackerspace or makerspace, you're invited to join us exploring a open way of community building and management.
